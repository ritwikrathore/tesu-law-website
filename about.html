About Tesu Law

A new-age law firm built on the principles of excellence, integrity, and client-centric service.

Our Story

Built for the Modern Business Landscape

Tesu Law was founded with a simple belief: legal services should be as dynamic and forward-thinking as the businesses they serve. Our founders, Raghav Bansal and Pranav Butalia, spent years at India's most prestigious law firms, advising on high-stakes matters and witnessing firsthand what clients truly need from their legal partners.

What they observed was a gap between traditional legal practice and the rapidly evolving needs of modern enterprises. Startups needed advisors who understood their unique challenges. Established businesses needed counsel that moved at the pace of commerce. Investors needed partners who could see both the legal and commercial picture.

Tesu Law was created to fill this gap. We combine the rigor and expertise of premier legal practice with the agility and commercial awareness that today's business environment demands. Our team brings deep experience from landmark Supreme Court cases to complex cross-border transactions, yet we approach every engagement with fresh thinking and a genuine commitment to our clients' success.

The name "Tesu" is derived from the flame-of-the-forest tree, known for its vibrant blossoms and resilience. Like our namesake, we aim to bring energy and strength to every matter we handle, helping our clients flourish in an increasingly complex legal landscape.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional legal services that empower businesses to achieve their objectives. We are committed to providing practical, commercially-aware advice that helps our clients navigate complexity with confidence. Every engagement is an opportunity to build lasting partnerships based on trust, transparency, and results.

Our Vision

To be the trusted legal partner for India's most ambitious companies and entrepreneurs. We envision a practice where legal excellence meets entrepreneurial thinking, where responsiveness is the norm rather than the exception, and where every client receives the dedicated attention their matters deserve.

Our Values

The Principles That Guide Us

These core values shape how we work, how we serve our clients, and how we build our firm.

Excellence

We hold ourselves to the highest standards of legal practice. Every opinion, every document, every strategy reflects our commitment to quality. We never compromise on thoroughness or accuracy.

Client Partnership

We see ourselves as partners in our clients' success, not just service providers. We invest in understanding their business, their industry, and their goals. Their priorities become our priorities.

Responsiveness

Business doesn't wait, and neither should legal counsel. We pride ourselves on being accessible and responsive, providing timely advice when our clients need it most.

Integrity

Trust is the foundation of effective legal counsel. We are committed to transparency, honesty, and ethical practice in everything we do. Our reputation is built on doing right by our clients.

Innovation

We embrace new approaches and continuously seek better ways to serve our clients. Whether it's leveraging technology or rethinking traditional processes, we stay ahead of the curve.

Commercial Pragmatism

Legal advice must make business sense. We focus on practical solutions that advance our clients' commercial objectives, not just technically correct opinions that sit unused.
