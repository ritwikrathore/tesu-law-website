Common Questions

Before You Reach Out

What should I prepare for an initial consultation?

It's helpful to have a brief summary of your situation and any relevant documents. For corporate matters, this might include existing agreements, corporate documents, or term sheets. For disputes, any correspondence or documentation related to the issue would be useful. Don't worry if you don't have everything organized, we can help you identify what's needed.

How do you structure your fees?

We offer flexible fee arrangements depending on the nature of the engagement. This may include hourly rates, fixed fees for defined scopes of work, or retainer arrangements for ongoing advisory relationships. We're always transparent about costs and will discuss fee structures during our initial conversation.

Do you work with clients outside Delhi?

Yes, we work with clients across India and internationally. Many of our engagements are conducted remotely through video calls, email, and document sharing. For matters requiring court appearances, we have networks across jurisdictions to ensure effective representation.

How quickly can you respond to urgent matters?

We understand that legal matters can be time-sensitive. For urgent issues, please indicate this in your message or call us directly. We make every effort to respond to urgent matters same-day and can accommodate expedited timelines when necessary.
