Our Practice Areas

Comprehensive legal solutions tailored to the needs of modern businesses, entrepreneurs, and investors.

At Tesu Law, we provide end-to-end legal support across the full business lifecycle. Whether you're launching a startup, raising capital, protecting intellectual property, or resolving complex disputes, our experienced team delivers strategic counsel that advances your commercial objectives.

Startup & Founder Advisory

Launching and scaling a business requires more than a great idea. We provide comprehensive legal support to founders at every stage, from initial incorporation to successful exit. Our deep understanding of the startup ecosystem means we anticipate challenges before they arise and structure your company for long-term success.

  • Company incorporation and entity selection (Private Limited, LLP, Partnership)
  • Founder agreements and equity structuring
  • Employee stock option plans (ESOPs) design and implementation
  • Co-founder disputes and exit arrangements
  • Corporate governance and board advisory
  • Business restructuring and exit strategies

Representative Work

Advised multiple early-stage startups on incorporation, founder equity arrangements, and initial funding rounds. Structured ESOPs for technology companies to attract and retain talent while maintaining tax efficiency.

Venture Capital & Funding

Capital raising is a critical milestone for growing companies. We represent both founders and investors in equity and debt transactions, ensuring that deal terms protect your interests while enabling the partnership to succeed. Our experience spans seed rounds to Series C and beyond.

  • Term sheet negotiation and structuring
  • Equity financing (seed, Series A/B/C rounds)
  • Convertible instruments (SAFE, convertible notes)
  • Venture debt and debt financing
  • Due diligence coordination
  • Investor rights and shareholder agreements
  • Down rounds and bridge financing

Representative Work

Advised on equity and debt transactions exceeding INR 500 crore in aggregate value. Represented both startups and institutional investors in funding rounds across technology, fintech, and consumer sectors.

Intellectual Property

Your intellectual property is often your most valuable asset. We help clients develop comprehensive IP strategies that protect innovations, brands, and creative works. From registration to enforcement, we ensure your IP portfolio supports your business objectives.

  • Trademark registration and portfolio management
  • Copyright protection and licensing
  • Trade secret protection strategies
  • IP due diligence for transactions
  • Infringement analysis and enforcement
  • Technology licensing agreements
  • IP aspects of employment and contractor agreements

Representative Work

Developed IP protection strategies for technology startups and creative businesses. Handled trademark registrations across multiple classes and advised on IP licensing arrangements for software and content companies.

Commercial Contracts

Well-drafted contracts are the foundation of successful business relationships. We bring deep experience in negotiating and drafting high-value agreements across industries, particularly in heavily-regulated sectors where precision is critical.

  • Commercial agreements (supply, distribution, services)
  • Technology contracts (SaaS, licensing, development)
  • Joint venture and collaboration agreements
  • Procurement and vendor contracts
  • Employment and contractor agreements
  • Non-disclosure and confidentiality agreements
  • Contract review and risk assessment

Representative Work

Drafted and negotiated high-value commercial contracts in sectors including technology, financial services, and infrastructure. Advised on complex multi-party arrangements and long-term service agreements.

Dispute Resolution & Litigation

When disputes arise, you need advocates who understand both the law and your business. Our litigation team brings experience from India's highest courts, including appearances in landmark Supreme Court cases. We combine aggressive representation with strategic thinking to achieve optimal outcomes.

  • Commercial litigation before High Courts and Supreme Court
  • Domestic and international arbitration
  • Contractual disputes and breach claims
  • Shareholder and partnership disputes
  • Debt recovery and insolvency proceedings
  • Interim relief and injunctions
  • Alternative dispute resolution and mediation

Representative Work

Appeared in Constitution Bench matters before the Supreme Court including the Sabarimala Temple Review Petition, challenge to NEET, challenge to the IBC 2016, and challenge to RBI's stressed assets circular.

Regulatory Compliance

Operating in regulated sectors requires constant vigilance and expert guidance. We help businesses navigate complex regulatory frameworks, ensure ongoing compliance, and engage effectively with regulators. Our team stays current on regulatory developments to provide proactive advice.

  • Fintech and payments regulation (RBI compliance)
  • Foreign exchange and FEMA compliance
  • Data protection and privacy (DPDP Act)
  • Corporate compliance and secretarial matters
  • Environmental and sustainability compliance
  • Regulatory filings and approvals
  • Compliance audits and risk assessment

Representative Work

Advised fintech companies on regulatory compliance including RBI licensing requirements and payment aggregator regulations. Counseled on FEMA compliance for cross-border investments and foreign subsidiary structures.
